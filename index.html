At Mohr Good Ideas we are invested in the latest Branding ideas. We offer 3m reflective, neon, thermo flock, Glow, hologram, metallic, and opaque imprinting. With the expert help of our Suppliers we determine the best technique for your promotional needs. We are proud of our professional digitizers who ensure that the details of your logo and message are pain-stakingly communicated with each piece. Embroidery can mean 3D puff, chenille and crests - we do it all. Laser etching and debossing are stunning methods of decoration that impress and communicate great attention to detail. Do you need labels? We have woven or PVC. Customizing a look for your Company - it's not a problem for Mohr Good Ideas - we work with talented designers and love a challenge.
At Mohr Good Ideas, when it comes to Eco-friendly promotional products, we do our best to 'WALK OUR TALK'. We choose responsibly and keep up to date with our Vendors. We comply with the Quality Certification Alliance to ensure we are not consciously leaving a mess for someone else to clean up. We believe in reducing our carbon footprint and reward those Vendors with the same objectives by placing our orders with them. On our web site look for the icons symbolizing Eco-friendly and please don't hesitate to give us your Eco-friendly choices.
We love donating to the Embrace Rwanda Program. After the genocide in Rwanda in the late 90's that all important factor in a young mother's life was missing. Her Mother, Grandmother and Aunties had been killed in the massive slayings. That left young mothers without the natural advice and mentoring the elder generations passed on to ensure survival. Recognizing the huge deficit an ex nurse stepped in to quietly and effectively bring about life-giving changes. At present we support Hilary King with Gifts and samples to the people of Rwanda. We know that we are only one drop in the bucket when it comes to stepping into the gap. www.embracerwanda.org
